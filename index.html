Project Title & Description
Project goal and overview

Project Title: Personal Cybersecurity Awareness Guide

A practical, easy-to-understand guide that helps individuals recognize and protect themselves from common online security threats. The final product will combine research-based content with user-friendly explanations and tips for daily online safety.

🎯
Vision, Mission & Strategy Alignment
How mission maps to goal
▼

Personal Mission Connection:

The mission of my project is to create a practical and easy-to-understand cybersecurity awareness guide that helps individuals protect themselves online. Through clear explanations and actionable advice, my goal is to simplify complex cybersecurity topics so that anyone, regardless of technical background, can learn how to stay safe in the digital world.

Vision Alignment:

The vision of this project is to build a more informed and secure online community where people feel confident about their digital safety. I aim to empower individuals to take control of their online presence and make smarter, safer choices while using technology.

Strategic Goal Support:

This project supports my mission to help others become more informed and confident about online safety. By researching cybersecurity threats and presenting them in a clear, accessible format, I am aligning my personal goals with the broader objective of promoting digital literacy and responsible technology use. The project also supports strategic learning goals by strengthening my skills in research, communication, and design while creating a resource that benefits others.

💼
Business Case & Justification
Why this project matters
▼

Professional Value:

This project provides strong professional value by helping me develop skills that are highly relevant in today’s digital environment. Researching cybersecurity topics improves my ability to analyze credible sources, synthesize information, and communicate technical ideas clearly. Designing and organizing the guide enhances my digital literacy, content development, and visual communication skills, all of which are valuable in both academic and professional settings.

Personal Value:

On a personal level, this project increases my awareness of online safety and helps me apply protective habits in my daily life. It encourages responsible technology use and builds my confidence in navigating digital platforms securely. Creating this guide also gives me a sense of purpose, knowing that the resource I produce can help others protect their personal information and make safer online decisions.

Learning Outcomes:

Through this project, I expect to gain a deeper understanding of cybersecurity concepts and the ability to explain them in a way that is clear and engaging for a general audience. I will strengthen my research, writing, and design skills, as well as learn to manage a project from planning to completion. By the end of the semester, I will have produced a professional-quality guide that demonstrates both my knowledge and my ability to apply project management principles effectively.

High-Level Scope
In / Out / Roadmap
▼

✅ In Scope for Semester Project

  • Research and explain safe browsing habits, including recognizing secure websites, avoiding malicious links, and understanding privacy settings.
  • identify common signs of phishing emails and messages, and provide clear steps for verifying legitimate sources.
  • Emphasize the importance of creating strong, unique passwords and introduce methods for safely managing them (e.g., password managers).
  • Write, design, and format the Personal Cybersecurity Awareness Guide into a clear and visually engaging document.

🗺️ Roadmap Items (≤6 Months)

  • Add more advanced interactive learning features (e.g., progress tracking or achievements).
  • Expand the content library with new topics such as social media security and data privacy.
  • Translate the guide into other languages to reach a wider audience.
  • Publish and promote the website on professional platforms (e.g., LinkedIn, GitHub, portfolio site).

❌ Out of Scope

  • Advanced cybersecurity training or hands-on coding tutorials.
  • Implementation of real cybersecurity tools or monitoring systems.
  • Large-scale marketing or turning the project into a national awareness campaign.
