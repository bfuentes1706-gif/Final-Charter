    Project Title: Personal Cybersecurity Awareness Guide

    This project will create a clear and accessible cybersecurity guide that teaches everyday internet users how to protect themselves from common online threats such as phishing, weak passwords, and unsafe browsing. The guide will provide step-by-step tips, examples, and best practices to promote safer digital habits. It will benefit students, professionals, and the general public by increasing awareness and reducing the risk of cyberattacks.
